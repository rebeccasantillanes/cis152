Our Story

Pacific Trails Resort is a family business, was founded over two decades ago. Since the beginning the goal has been to design a more sophisticated way to experience the wild, where luxury and nature meet. While keeping the essence of our roots, we have evolved through the years aiming to design a home away from home for our guests. We offer a high-end experience while remaining eco-friendly, we make sure to take care of all the details of your stay so you are able to fully immerse yourself in the beauty of nature. We offer private yurts, a campsite with ocean view which helps to enhance your experience allowing you to comfortably enjoy nature.
